How It Works

The Realty Referral Resource (RRR) Referral Associate first submits a buyer or seller referral through the RRR portal. From there, RRR will reach out to a partner agent in the locale of the referral. The Receiving Agent will then service the client with diligence and professionalism, as stipulated in the RRR Referral Agreement.The Referral Associate will not be a direct part of the transaction process, but he/she will receive periodic updates regarding the transaction progress via email.Upon completion of the transaction, RRR will receive the referral portion of the commission from the receiving brokerage, and the RRR Referral Associate will havehis/her referral fee direct-deposited within a matter of days.
