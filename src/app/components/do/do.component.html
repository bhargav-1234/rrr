What Does A Referral AssosiateDo?

❖ Maintains a current real estate license.

❖ Completes all required Continuing Education requirements to keep license current.

❖ Renew license as scheduled according to local requirements.

❖ Submits referral clients through the RRR portal.

What Does A Referral Assosiate NotDo?

❖ Make appointments, Show property

❖ Provide advice to Buyers or Sellers regarding price, negotiating strategy, or repair strategy

❖ Write offers, Attend any inspections

❖ Interfere with any active real estate professional

❖ Pay fees to any local real estate board, MLS, or Realtor Association

❖ Pay E&O
